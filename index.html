About us

Introducing Stringent, the Dutch metal sensation hailing from Gouda and its surroundings. Their focus on groove and melody creates irresistibly catchy riffs, while the drums promise a neck-breaking experience. As a bonus, their exceptional female growler on vocals will redefine your understanding of intensity.

With influences from black metal and progressive metal(core), Stringent crafts a unique blend that guarantees an unforgettable auditory journey. Expect a sonic onslaught that doesn't sacrifice the energy for circle pits or leave your neck pain-free the next morning.

Join Stringent in the pit and immerse yourself in a raw, powerful experience where every growl and riff, delivered by their exceptional female vocalist, resonates with unbridled intensity. See you in the pit, where the extraordinary awaits!
